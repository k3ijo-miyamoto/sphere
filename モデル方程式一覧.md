# Sphere モデル方程式一覧

このファイルは、実装済みの主要方程式を一覧化したカタログです。

## 1. 潜在歪み（Strain）更新

\[
\text{strain}_{t+1}=\mathrm{clip}_{[0,1]}\left(\text{strain}_t(1-\delta)+k_a S_t+0.03 D_t-k_r R_t\right)
\]

- \(\delta\): `decayPerTick`
- \(k_a\): `addScale`
- \(k_r\): `reliefScale`
- \(S_t\): shock intensity
- \(R_t\): recovery
- \(D_t\): policy displacement

参照: `src/sim/cityDynamics.js` `updateCityStrainAndRegime`

## 2. Shock Intensity

\[
S_t=\mathrm{clip}\left(
0.24U+0.18P+0.18I+0.12Q+0.14(1-T)+0.08R_s+0.06G+0.10Y,
0,1.8
\right)
\]

- \(U\): unemployment
- \(P\): price stress
- \(I\): instability
- \(Q\): inequality
- \(T\): trust
- \(R_s\): resource stress
- \(G\): geopolitical shock
- \(Y\): income stress

参照: `src/sim/cityDynamics.js` `updateCityStrainAndRegime`

## 3. Recovery

\[
R_t=\mathrm{clip}\left(0.32\,safety+0.24\,trust+0.18\,productivity+0.16(1-U)+0.10(1-R_s),0,1.6\right)
\]

参照: `src/sim/cityDynamics.js` `updateCityStrainAndRegime`

## 4. レジーム遷移（ヒステリシス）

- `normal -> stressed`: \(strain > T_1\)
- `stressed -> fractured`: \(strain > T_2\)
- `stressed -> normal`: \(strain < T_{1b}\)
- `fractured -> stressed`: \(strain < T_{2b}\)

既定値（config）:
- \(T_1=0.42\), \(T_2=0.72\), \(T_{1b}=0.32\), \(T_{2b}=0.58\)

参照: `src/config/defaultConfig.js`, `src/sim/cityDynamics.js`

## 5. レジーム効果（都市指標）

\[
trust \leftarrow \mathrm{clip}(trust-(1-m_t)\cdot0.006-0.0015\cdot strain)
\]
\[
instability \leftarrow \mathrm{clip}(instability\cdot(0.985+0.05(m_i-1))+0.005\cdot strain)
\]
\[
employmentCapacity \leftarrow \mathrm{clip}(employmentCapacity\cdot m_h-0.01\cdot strain)
\]

参照: `src/sim/cityDynamics.js` `applyRegimeEffectsToCity`

## 6. 移住発火確率（Regime/Strain連動）

\[
p_{move} \propto baseRate \cdot drive \cdot cooldown \cdot fatigue \cdot M_{regime}\cdot (1+0.28\cdot strain)
\]

- \(M_{regime}\): normal=1.0, stressed=1.22, fractured=1.55

参照: `src/sim/population.js` `applyRelocationMigration`

## 7. 都市効用（移住選好）

\[
U_{city} \leftarrow U_{city} -0.12\cdot strain - \pi_{regime}
\]

- \(\pi_{regime}\): normal=0, stressed=0.08, fractured=0.18

参照: `src/sim/population.js` `cityUtility`

## 8. 雇用回復

\[
hireChance = \mathrm{clip}(hireBase \cdot m_h \cdot (1-0.15\cdot strain))
\]

参照: `src/sim/population.js` `applyEmploymentAndEconomy`

## 9. 企業回復（費用・資本）

\[
cost \leftarrow 0.82\,cost + 0.18\,(costTick + fixedCost\cdot(2-m_h))
\]
\[
capital \leftarrow \mathrm{clip}(capital + 0.01\,profit\cdot m_h \cdot (1-\mathrm{clip}(0.35\cdot strain,0,0.45)))
\]

参照: `src/sim/population.js` `applyEmploymentAndEconomy`

## 10. 都市資源政策（Contextual Bandit + Genome）

行動選択スコア:
\[
score(a)=Q(s,a)+GenomeBias(a)
\]

更新（評価窓後）:
\[
Q \leftarrow Q + \alpha(\bar r - Q)
\]

参照: `src/sim/cityDynamics.js` `chooseResourcePolicyAction`, `updateResourcePolicyLearning`

## 11. 企業RL（Tabular Q(s,a)）

行動選択: ε-greedy on \(Q(s,a)\)

更新:
\[
Q(s,a) \leftarrow Q(s,a)+\alpha(r-Q(s,a))
\]

報酬は profit, marketShare, capital, distress, employment stability の混合。

参照: `src/sim/population.js` `applyCompanyRlActions`, `updateCompanyRlLearning`

## 12. 投資RL（Regime-aware）

- 状態: `holderType | regime | riskBand`
- 更新は日次ではなく評価窓（日単位間隔）で集約

更新:
\[
Q \leftarrow Q + (\alpha \cdot scale_{regime})(\bar r - Q)
\]

参照: `src/sim/population.js` `simulateInstitutionalInvestments`, `detectInvestmentRegime`

## 13. 外交RL（低頻度更新）

状態: `status/tension/dependence/stress` を離散化。

更新は蓄積報酬を評価窓で反映:
\[
Q \leftarrow Q + \alpha(\bar r - Q)
\]

参照: `src/sim/geopolitics.js` `chooseDiplomacyAction`, `updateDiplomacyPolicyLearning`

## 14. 秘密結社RL（軽学習）

更新:
\[
Q_a \leftarrow 0.995\,Q_a \quad (\text{forgetting})
\]
\[
Q(a) \leftarrow Q(a)+\alpha(r-Q(a))
\]

参照: `src/sim/geopolitics.js` `updateSecretSocietyPolicyLearning`

## 15. 多層同時更新トリガ

\[
forceGeopoliticsTick = (\#regimeTransitions > 0) \lor \left(\frac{\#fractured}{\#cities} \ge \theta\right)
\]

- \(\theta\): `geopoliticalTriggerFracThreshold`（既定0.14）

参照: `src/sim/cityDynamics.js` `finalizeStrainMetrics`, `src/sim/engine.js`, `src/sim/geopolitics.js`
